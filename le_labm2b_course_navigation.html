M2B: Course Navigation

These are the three navigation items I have noticed most:

Class Progress
Class Progress is the most interesting because it shows all the statistics of all of my discussion posts and the grades I have received for quizzes and assignments. It also compares the total time I spend on this site to other members of the class.
Quizzes
Quizzes are a necessary item which I will use to access the quizzes. There is not much more to say about this Quizzes item except for that I will be accessing it at least once a week.
Discussions
The Discussions item is as important as the Quizzes item. Since much of the homework in this course is submitted using the Dicussion Board, I will find myself spending most of my time with this section of the course. I also have found some very helpful hints in this section.

These are the other helpful items on the site:

Setting
The Setting item allows me to change my profile. I have tried to explore it and was able to attach a picture for my avatar. However, I still do not know how to put a link of my email and message to my avatar like the other members had done. I will explore this function some more and will try to use it more effectively.
Bell
I am glad the Bell is present. When there is a reply to my post, the Bell notices me with a yellow sticker. When I click on it, it takes me directly to the reply. Without this feature, I would have to go digging around to see whether anyone has replied to my posts. That would be a bit time consuming.
Print
The Print item should allow me to print material from this site. I have not used it yet, but I am sure at some point, I will need to print some note from this course. With this function, I don't have to make copies of the text and then print it, I can print it directly from the page.
