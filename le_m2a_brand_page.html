Mission Statement and TagLine.

Mission

The Vegan Deer is a simple restaurant to serve vegan and vegetarian food to those who have passion for being a vegetarian or a vegan. The name Vegan Deer is chosen because the Deer is a gentle animal, and Deer also sounds like Dear.

TagLine
